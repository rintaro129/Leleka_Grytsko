Leleka Grytsko - Home page

The unique bookstore "Leleka Grytsko" offers a wide selection of Ukrainian literature books for both domestic and foreign audiences interested in and fascinated by Ukrainian culture, but unfortunately unable to immerse themselves in the world of Ukrainian literature due to the language barrier. "Leleka Grytsko" is not only a place for Ukrainian classical and contemporary literature but also for their translations. It is the only bookstore specializing in the distribution and publication of translations of Ukrainian books and the promotion of Ukrainian culture worldwide. Learn more...
