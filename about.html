About the Bookstore

The Unique Bookstore "Leleka Hrytsko"

The unique bookstore "Leleka Hrytsko" offers a wide selection of books of Ukrainian literature for foreign readers who admire and are interested in Ukrainian culture, but, unfortunately, are not able to plunge into the world of Ukrainian literature because of the language barrier. In the bookstore you will find both Ukrainian classical and contemporary literature. This is the only bookstore in the world that specializes in the distribution and publication of translations of Ukrainian books and the dissemination of Ukrainian culture around the world.

The Symbolism of the leleka (stork in ukrainian)

The stork is a bird that spends more than half of the year not in its homeland, but rather flies away. It is this bird that symbolizes our bookstore, because we are also stay abroad and introduce people to Ukrainian literature.

Hrytsko the Stork

Hrytsko the stork is a real stork that lives in the Pyriatynskyi National Park in the village of Leliaky in Poltava Oblast, and has gained immense popularity on the Internet thanks to his YouTube channel. On it, he conducts round-the-clock broadcast of his nest, and on March 19, 2024, he finally arrived in Ukraine!
